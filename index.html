I'm Chris - a lifelong lover of conversation, curiosity, and computers. Originally from the Greater Lakes region, I left my previous life in sales to move west and undertake fresh challenges. Now living with my wife in Ogden, UT, I'm enjoying a new chapter with beautiful mountain views.

For nearly twenty years, I was a top performer at specialty retailer GameStop. While regularly driving sales and training future leaders, my biggest accomplishment is delivering service ranked at the 99th percentile among all GameStop U.S. retail locations nationwide.

Having moved on to new challenges, I'm currently enrolled in the Weber State University School of Computing. Combining my lifelong interest in computers and a fondness for problem solving, I am working toward a Fall '24 graduation with my Bachelor of Science in Computer Science.
